Jiwan Lee

I am Jiwan Lee, a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science and Engineering at POSTECH, working under the supervision of Prof. Seungmoon Choi. My research investigates multisensory perception and haptic experience design, focusing on how sensory cues and their combinations are perceived and translated into authoring for improved experience quality. I am currently completing my doctoral research and seeking postdoctoral opportunities that build on my expertise in haptics, multisensory perception, and immersive experience design.
