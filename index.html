Curriculum Vitae

Jiwan Lee

I’m Jiwan Lee, set to receive a Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Seungmoon Choi at POSTECH. My research focuses on multisensory perception and haptic experience design. My work serves as a bridge between perception and design, offering practical insights for creating effective multisensory content in virtual reality and other immersive environments. I am open to exploring new research areas that leverage my expertise in sensory design and immersive experiences.
